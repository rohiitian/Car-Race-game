Instructions

  1. Use I J K L or arrow keys to operate the car.

  2. Yellow square are coins that you need to collect.

  3. Coins appear everytime when score is a multiple of 10

  4. Coins earned get collected over time as total coins

  5. Coins can be spent to slow down enemies and also your own car

  6. Use coins wisely as each time slowing requires 5 coins

  7. You can use P key to pause the game and C to continue
